GOA

Goal-Oriented Academy (GOA): Creating the optimal environment for children in Georgia, GOA is committed to providing classes that bring joy to every child. The anticipation of the next lesson unifies their spirits.

Together, we are shaping a bright future. Our principal mentor, Nika Keshelava, consistently prioritizes the spiritual and physical development of each child. He guides them on the right path and offers continuous assistance in problem-solving.

Parents of GOA children experience a sense of happiness and tranquility, knowing that their child is in reliable and caring hands.

Squad System

Squad System at Goal-Oriented Academy (GOA): Our innovative approach extends beyond the classroom with a unique squad system. In this system, each successful student is appointed as a leader and mentor within their own squad of fellow children. This not only fosters a sense of camaraderie but also provides valuable experience and motivation for all members.

By creating these student-led squads, we aim to empower our learners to take on leadership roles, sharing their knowledge and inspiring others. This collaborative structure enhances the overall learning environment, promoting mutual support and a shared journey toward success.
